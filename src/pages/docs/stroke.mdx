## 基本用法

### 设置描边颜色

使用`stroke-{color}`实用程序来更改SVG的笔画颜色。

```html {{ example: true }}
<div class="flex items-center justify-center">
  <svg class="stroke-cyan-500 h-10" viewBox="0 0 48 40" fill="none" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
    <path d="M1 13C1 10.2386 3.23858 8 6 8H13.4914C14.3844 8 15.1691 7.40805 15.4144 6.54944L16.5856 2.45056C16.8309 1.59196 17.6156 1 18.5086 1H29.4914C30.3844 1 31.1691 1.59195 31.4144 2.45056L32.5856 6.54944C32.8309 7.40804 33.6156 8 34.5086 8H42C44.7614 8 47 10.2386 47 13V34C47 36.7614 44.7614 39 42 39H6C3.23858 39 1 36.7614 1 34V13Z" stroke-width="2"/> <circle cx="24" cy="23" r="9" stroke-width="2"/>
  </svg>
</div>
```

```html
<svg class="**stroke-cyan-500** ...">
  <!-- ... -->
</svg>
```

这对于像[Heroicons](https://heroicons.com)这样的图标设置样式很有用。
